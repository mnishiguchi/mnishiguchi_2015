For the more curious...

What does Nishi mean?

It means the west in Japanese. Its kanji is 西.

What does Guchi mean?

Guchi is a variant of kuchi, which means a month or opening. Its kanji is 口.

What does Nishiguchi mean?

With Nishi and Guchi together, it means a west entrance/exit. You will see 西口 on signs at train stations in Japan.
